What do we do?

Machining

Machine and assemble parts of robots using tools like 3D printers and CNCs.

Design & CAD

Create designs using software like Onshape and Fusion360 for competition and trophies.

Programming

Write and maintain all code for robot operation. Our main language is Kotlin.

Electrical

Focus on wiring motors, soldering, and assembling electronic components.

Business

Manage logistics, fundraising, and present the Impact Award during competitions.

Media

Create and distribute content to promote our team during and outside competitions.
